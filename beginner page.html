Perfect for First-Time Dancers

Our beginner Garba classes are specially designed for those who have never danced before or have minimal experience. We break down each step slowly and focus on building your confidence along with your skills.

What You'll Learn

Basic Steps

Master the fundamental footwork patterns that form the foundation of Garba

Rhythm & Timing

Develop your sense of rhythm to dance in sync with traditional music

Hand Movements

Learn graceful hand gestures that complement the footwork

Your First Class

Here's what to expect when you join your first beginner Garba class:

  1. Warm welcome: We'll introduce you to the class and our teaching approach
  2. Gentle warm-up: Simple stretches to prepare your body
  3. Basic stance: Learning the proper posture for Garba
  4. First steps: Clapping rhythm with basic footwork
  5. Practice circle: Trying simple movements in a group circle
  6. Cool down: Wind down with stretching and questions

Beginner FAQs

Do I need any dance experience?

No prior experience is needed! We start with absolute basics and move at a pace comfortable for everyone.

What should I wear?

Comfortable clothes that allow movement, and shoes with good support (or barefoot if preferred). Traditional attire is not required.

What if I can't keep up?

Our instructors give individual attention and repeat steps as needed. Small class sizes ensure no one gets left behind.

How long until I see progress?

Most beginners pick up the basic steps within 2-3 classes and feel comfortable with simple Garba patterns after 4-6 weeks.
